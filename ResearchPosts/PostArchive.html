Strip The Stripes!

Artifact detection and removal for scanning electron microscopy images.

  • July 23, 2020

Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM) is a popular high resolution imaging modality for biological samples that has recently been applied to neural circuit reconstruction. We present an automatic pipeline designed to detect and correct for striping artifacts while minimally degrading the unknown artifact-free image.

Image Super-Resolution

Image super-resolution for acoustic microscopy!

  • July 23, 2020

Scanning acoustic microscopy (SAM) is an imaging modality used to obtain 2D maps of acoustical and mechanical properties of soft tissues and uses ultrasound transducers operating at very high-frequencies. The aim of this study is to introduce a bilateral approach that enhances the resolution of SAM images by leveraging the co-registered high-resolution histology image. We propose to use bilateral weighted total variation regularization to solve the super-resolution problem.
